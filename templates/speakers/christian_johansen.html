Speaker Profile

Christian Johansen

I am a software developer at the Norwegian Food Safety Authority where I build systems with Clojure and Datomic. I have 20 years of experience, and have been working full-time with Clojure for the last 10 of those. My career has focused on web tech, and love doing what it takes to ship working software to users: from setting up infrastructure to data modeling and frontend work.

I love sharing, and keep a blog (in Norwegian) with my co-workers, give frequent talks at conferences, used to teach courses, and maintain a hodge-podge of open source software libraries in the Clojure ecosystem.
