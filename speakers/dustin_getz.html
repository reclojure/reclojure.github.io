Speaker Profile

Dustin Getz

Dustin Getz is the founder of Hyperfiddle, a "UI Spreadsheet" for making internal app frontends. Hyperfiddle's mission is to collapse to zero the cost of internal frontend development for a wide range of business process applications, by applying distributed systems and concurrency techniques to build a new class of UI infrastructure.
