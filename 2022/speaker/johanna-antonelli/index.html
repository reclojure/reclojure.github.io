Johanna Antonelli

After earning a Masters degree in Physics, Johanna joined JUXT as a software developer. There, she was introduced to Clojure, both as a language and a community. Over the past few years, alongside client project work, she has been involved in training and mentoring, creating the XTDB "Space adventure" tutorial (https://github.com/xtdb/xtdb-tutorial). Johanna's passions include solving problems and exploring the English countryside with her husband and dog.

Schema driven development with graphql

By Johanna Antonelli
