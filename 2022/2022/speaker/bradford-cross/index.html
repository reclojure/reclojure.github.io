Bradford was the founder and CEO of Prismatic, an early Clojure startup that brought together some really great engineers under one roof. He's founded a number of successful startups in consumer, SaaS and enterprise across social media, fintech, and biotech. How startups can move fast with Clojure

In this talk, I'll outline how startups can leverage bottom-up programming in Clojure to build better products faster. I'll report on my experience picking Clojure for my startups over 10 years ago and why I'm doubling down on Clojure in 2022.
